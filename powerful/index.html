Program Length

If you start from A1 and complete all the levels up to C2, it will take 24 months.
Join us at any level and see your English improve step by step, a level in 4 months

A1 - BEGINNER

You will learn basic words and simple sentences to talk about yourself, your family, and everyday life. You can introduce yourself, ask and answer simple questions, and understand very basic conversations.

A2 - ELEMENTARY

You will build on your basic English skills. You can talk about daily routines, shopping, work, and past experiences. You can understand and use simple phrases to communicate in common situations.

B1 - INTER.

You can have longer conversations and understand simple texts. You will learn to express opinions, describe experiences, and handle everyday situations like traveling, ordering food, and making appointments.

B2 - UPPER INTER.

You can speak more confidently and understand more complex ideas. You can talk about work, studies, and social topics. You will improve your grammar and vocabulary to sound more natural..

C1 - ADVANCED

You can understand difficult texts and speak fluently in many situations. You will learn to express yourself clearly and persuasively in conversations, presentations, and writing.

C2 - PROFICIENCY

You can speak and write English like a native speaker. You can understand everything, including complex texts and fast conversations. You will polish your grammar and vocabulary for professional and academic use.
