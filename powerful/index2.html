About Powerful English Institute


Powerful English Institute was founded by Johnsley MEJUSTIN, an English enthusiast. He has a TikTok page, a YouTube channel, and you can also follow him on Facebook. Johnsley’s goal is to help more people around the world learn and improve their English.
